Makeup

Makeup has evolved from years until now. People didnt wear makeup until the 1920'2. During the 1920's the girls would wear thin arched black eyebrows, heart shaped lips, pink blush in a circle on cheecks, and smudged eyeshadow. In addition, centuries ago women used burnt matches to darken their eyes, berries to stain their lips and young boys' urine to fade their freckles. By the 1920s, every pharmacy and department store had there makeup counters. More makeup was being made during the years making it more better, and at the beginning the makeup was becoming more refined on women

Now in 2016 makeup has evolved into many ways. Lots of young girls are wearing makeup due to the common name "Fleeky". Now with so much technology girls are using youtube to make or watch makeup tutorials. Contouring is a ashade darker than yours and a color about 2 tones lighter than yours. This technique makes your face pop out, define, or reshape any part of your face. Eyebrows have also become a popular thing in which girls have fleek(on point) eyebrows by shaping them out with either a brow pencil, brow gel, or any other product to shape your eyebrows. Eyeshadow and eyeliner is also another trend the eyeshadow must be blended right and then comes the sharp eyeliner, and if you prefer using mascara or eyelashes either is fine.
